ABOUT

WASSEF is a multi-disciplinary architecture and engineering company based in Cairo, Egypt. Since 1978,
we have been committed to providing high quality professional services in the fields of architecture,
structural and civil engineering, landscape design, interiors, construction consultation,
and project management, to a wide range of domestic and international clients in several industries,
from educational institutions and commercial developments, to remodelling and residential projects.
